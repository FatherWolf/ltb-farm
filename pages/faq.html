FAQ

Do you train?

No. We are not a training facility. We only board. We are a non judgemental place for horse enthusiasts of all levels to board and ride. You may bring your trainer to our barn

Can I bring my trainer?

Absolutely, There are no restrictions with trainers comming to the facility

Can visitors come?

Yes, visitors are allowed and they must sign a waiver located in the barn. Boarders assume responsibility for all visitors. Visitors less than 18 must remain with boarder at all times.

Do you charge fees?

Board price includes everything except a surcharge for hay depending on hay pricing and the year. This is discussed during the free tour and is very reasonable. We do not charge holding fees for farriers or vets, nor do we charge for applying a fly mask or blanket. Please note however all vet fees and farrier fees are the responsibility of the boarder.

What is needed to board?

We meet with all potential borders and horses first. We offer a free farm tour, as we want you to know exactly what you are getting and to discuss expectations. Additionally, a negative Coggins is required. We also require all horses are seen by a vet (you can use yours or we can get you set up with a local vet here) once a year or more often depending on health. Also all horses must been seen by a farrier for hoof trimming or shoeing and hoof inspection. This will be discussed during the tour. We can assist with holding your horse for the vet or farrier.
